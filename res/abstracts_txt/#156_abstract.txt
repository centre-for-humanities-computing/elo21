This workshop presents a hands-on introduction to the RiTa v2.0 tools, including the new RiScript
language. Version 2 of RiTa is a complete rewrite of the library that is easier-to-use, faster and more
powerful. The workshop will cover the basics of RiTa and RiScript in JavaScript, with a specific focus on
the Observable notebook environment.
